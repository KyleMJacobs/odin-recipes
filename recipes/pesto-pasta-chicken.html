Pesto Pasta with Chicken

Description

This quick and easy recipe is a delicious use of pesto sauce! It's filling, tasty, and simple to make.

This recipe requires about 10 minutes of prep time (it'll be higher if you decide to make fresh pesto yourself). Cook time will be about 20 minues, for a combined (prep and cook) time of about 30 minues. It'll serve approximately 8 people.

Ingredients

  • 16oz package of bow tie pasta
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic (2 cloves if you're mincing it yourself)
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cubed
  • Crushed red pepper flakes to taste
  • 1/2 cup pesto sauce (learn how to make your own here)
  • 1/3 cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips
