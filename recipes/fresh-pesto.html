Fresh Pesto

Description

Fresh pesto is easy and delicious to make! It goes well with many things, but esspecially well with pasta. It's often added to chicken as well.

Pesto can be made in two main ways. With a food processor, which is quicker and easier. Or without a food processor, (usually with mortar and pestle, but sometimes just finally chopped), which is slower, but often is more flavorfull.

Ingredients

  • A few cups of fresh basil
  • 3-4 peeled cloves of garlic
  • Freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Pine nuts

Steps

With a food processor

  1. Add basil, garlic cloves, pine nuts, and parmesan cheese to food processor
  2. Begin blending in the food processor
  3. Slowly pour in olive oil while mixing
  4. When it becomes the desiered paste-to-sauce-like consistancey, add salt and pepper to personal taste

Without a food processor

  1. Add basil, garlic cloves, pine nuts, and parmesan cheese to mortar
  2. Begin slowly grinding and mixing the ingredients with the pestle
  3. Slowly add olive oil as you continue to mix
  4. Once it reaches your desired consistancy, add salt and pepper to taste
